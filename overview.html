Online Examination System

Now-a-days, Online examination system has become popular for competitive examinations because of its unique features such as auto-evaluation, speed and accuracy. In such a system, students are asked to select answers from multiple options given for a single question. Likewise, there are several questions which appear in the studentsâ€™ systems. The questions and multiple options are saved in a database along with desired answers. Typically, a student can change an answer after saving it, however, cannot change after submitting the answer. Administrator has special access in the website. The administrator can create, modify and delete questions and accordingly, the question is updated in the website.

Role of Administrator

  • The administrator will have a unique email and password.
  • He/she should login with those credentials
  • The administrator can now Create, Modify or Delete questions.
  • Accordingly the questions will be updated in the website.

Role of Student

  • A New user should Sign Up by providing all the details required.
  • An Existing User should signin by providing Email and Password.
  • The user can take the tests provided in the website.
  • The student should save the answers and submit the exam at the end.

After Completion of exam..

  • Result of the student will be displayed.
  • The results will be displayed in descending order according to obtained marks with Roll Number.
  • Some other analytics of all students will be displayed.
